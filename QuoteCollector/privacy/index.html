QuoteCollector Privacy Policy

Overview

QuoteCollector helps you save and organize your favorite quotes. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. The short answer: we don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device or in your iCloud account.

Data We Store

QuoteCollector stores the following information locally on your device:

  • • Quote text and authors
  • • Like counts and favorites
  • • Tags and categories
  • • Pinned quote preferences

All of this data is stored on your device or synchronized through your personal iCloud account using CloudKit. We do not have access to any of this information.

CloudKit Sync

QuoteCollector uses Apple's CloudKit to synchronize your data across your devices. This synchronization is handled entirely through Apple's iCloud service using your Apple ID. We do not access, process, or store your data on our servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any other data collection mechanisms. Your privacy is our priority.
