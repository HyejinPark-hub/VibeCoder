Draw On Video

Privacy Policy

Overview

Draw On Video does not collect any personal data. Your privacy is respected by design.

Local Storage

The app stores playback positions locally on your device so you can resume videos where you left off. This data never leaves your device and is automatically cleaned up after 40 days.

Web Content

When you load websites within the app (such as YouTube), those sites may have their own privacy policies and tracking. Draw On Video does not control or monitor this content.

Third-Party Services

Draw On Video uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in their privacy policy.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve these ads, Google may collect or receive certain information about your device and usage. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Contact
