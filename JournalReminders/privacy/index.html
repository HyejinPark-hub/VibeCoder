Privacy Policy for Journal Reminders

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Journal Reminders is an app designed to help you organize your schedule. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data you create (reminders, templates) is stored locally on your device.

Calendar Access

The app requests access to your Calendar to create reminders from your calendar events. This process happens entirely on your device. We do not transmit your calendar data to any external servers.

Notifications

The app uses local and remote notifications to remind you of your scheduled tasks. These notifications are managed by the operating system.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will post changes here.
