Privacy Policy for Expiration Date

Last updated: January 12, 2026

Expiration Date ("the App") is developed by VibeCoder.

Information We Collect

Camera

The App uses your device's camera to take photos of food items. These photos are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your account if you sign in.

Account Data

If you sign in with Google, we store your email address and food item data to enable sync across devices.

Ads

The free version displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of tracking food expiration dates
  • To sync your data across devices when signed in
  • To display relevant advertisements (free version only)

Data Storage

Your food items and photos are stored locally on your device. If you sign in, data is synced to Firebase (Google's cloud service).

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (data sync)
  • Google AdMob (advertisements)
  • Google Play Billing (purchases)

These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or signing out and clearing app data.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: vibecoder.company@gmail.com

Changes
