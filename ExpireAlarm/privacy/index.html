ExpireAlarm Privacy Policy

Data Collection

ExpireAlarm does not collect any private data separately. All data is stored locally on your device or in your iCloud storage.

Data Storage

All information you enter into ExpireAlarm, including expiration dates and reminders, is stored exclusively on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

ExpireAlarm may use iCloud for data synchronization across your devices. This is handled entirely through Apple's iCloud service, and we do not access or process this data.
