Simple_Todos Privacy Policy

Overview

Simple_Todos helps you manage your daily tasks and reminders. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. The short answer: we don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device or in your iCloud account.

Data We Store

Simple_Todos stores the following information locally on your device:

  • • Task titles and notes
  • • Due dates and times
  • • Task completion status
  • • Pinned task preferences
  • • Alarm and reminder settings

All of this data is stored on your device or synchronized through your personal iCloud account using CloudKit. We do not have access to any of this information.

CloudKit Sync

Simple_Todos uses Apple's CloudKit to synchronize your data across your devices. This synchronization is handled entirely through Apple's iCloud service using your Apple ID. We do not access, process, or store your data on our servers.

Notifications

Simple_Todos sends local notifications to remind you about your tasks. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely by iOS on your device. We do not have access to notification delivery data.

Advertising

Free users may see banner ads provided by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). These ads are displayed within the app interface. Google may collect data for ad personalization and measurement as described in their privacy policy. We do not share your personal data with Google for advertising purposes.

Pro users who upgrade to remove ads will not see any advertisements.

Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any other data collection mechanisms. Your privacy is our priority.

In-App Purchases

Simple_Todos offers optional upgrades through Apple's in-app purchase system. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.
