Privacy Policy for Simple Clock

Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

Simple Clock is an analog clock app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app stores minimal data locally on your device only:

  • • Your purchase status (if you bought "Remove Ads")
  • • Calendar sync preferences (if enabled)

This data never leaves your device.

Calendar Access

If you enable calendar sync, the app reads your calendar events to display them on the clock. Calendar data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect data as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can remove ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). We do not have access to your payment information.

Contact Support

If you use the "Contact Support" feature, your email is sent directly to us. We only use this for responding to your inquiry.

Third-Party Services

  • • Google AdMob - Advertising
  • • Apple App Store (iOS) / Google Play Store (Android) - In-app purchases

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will post changes here.
