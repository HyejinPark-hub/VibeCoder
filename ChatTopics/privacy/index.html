Privacy Policy for ChatTopics

Last Updated: February 2025

Overview

ChatTopics helps you track conversation topics with people in your life. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data you create (topics, people, and categories) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your personal iCloud account.

Data Storage

All app data including topics, people, and categories is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not have access to your data.

CloudKit Sync

The app uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices via your personal iCloud account. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you of conversation topics. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following Apple services:

  • • iCloud (CloudKit) for data sync across your devices
  • • App Store for app distribution

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will post changes here.
